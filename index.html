Terry O'Malley

I am an economist in the Central Bank of Ireland, where I mostly work on empirical financial stability topics. My research interests are in household finance, including mortgage choice and default.

I recently completed a PhD in economics at University College Dublin, where I was supervised by Prof Morgan Kelly.

There's me in the centre, between John McHale and John Muellbauer, receiving the 2017 Conniffe prize at the IEA annual conference.
